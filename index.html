Retail Analytics Case Study

An in-depth analysis of retail sales, customer behavior, product performance, and operational efficiency using advanced Tableau dashboards.

Problem Statement

Businesses often lack a unified view of sales, customers, products, and operations, making it difficult to make informed data-driven decisions.

Dataset details

  • Source: Retail dataset (academic capstone)
  • Contains: Orders, Customers, Products, Revenue
  • Scope: Multi-dimensional analysis

Data Cleaning & Transformation

  • Removed null values in crucial fields
  • Calculated fields (AOV, Success Rate)
  • Standardized shipping & customer categories
  • Aggregated core KPIs for dashboards

Key Insights

  • Revenue peaks strongly in April and August
  • Regular customers generate ~48% of total revenue
  • Weekday sales noticeably outperform weekends
  • Same-Day shipping yields the highest revenue margins

Recommendations

  • Focus on retaining regular customers via loyalty programs
  • Optimize same-day delivery logistics to maximize revenue
  • Improve pending order processing to reduce delays
  • Leverage identified peak months for targeted marketing
